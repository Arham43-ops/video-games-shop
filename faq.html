Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GameHub

Account & Registration

Creating a GameHub account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page, fill in your details, and follow the verification process. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a secure password.

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see our email.

Log in to your account and go to "Account Settings." From there, you can update your personal information, change your password, and manage your communication preferences.

Orders & Payments

We accept various payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • GameHub Gift Cards
  • Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, Ethereum)

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order by clicking the tracking link in the email or by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it, as long as it hasn't been processed for shipping. After that, please contact our customer support team immediately, and we'll do our best to assist you.

Shipping & Delivery

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days ($4.99)
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days ($9.99)
  • Next Day Shipping: 1 business day ($19.99)
  • Free Shipping: On orders over $49 (5-7 business days)

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. You'll see the available shipping options and costs at checkout before completing your purchase.

Please contact our customer support team within 7 days of receiving your order. Include your order number, photos of the damaged/incorrect items, and a brief description of the issue. We'll work quickly to resolve the problem.

Returns & Refunds

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most unopened, unused items in their original packaging. Digital products, including game codes and digital downloads, are generally not eligible for return unless required by law.

To start a return, please contact our customer support team with your order details.

Once we receive your returned item, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 7-14 business days
